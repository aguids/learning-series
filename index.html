RMU Learning Series

Walk-throughs of practical Ruby problems and their solutions

The Ruby Mendicant University (RMU) was founded by "Ruby Best Practices" author, Gregory Brown. Its purpose is not only to provide top-notch online training courses, but also to serve as a learning resource for intermediate Ruby developers. In order to gain alumni status, students must complete a variety of non-trivial ruby assignments. This ongoing series of mini e-books takes a detailed comparative look at some of the solutions submitted by our alumni.
